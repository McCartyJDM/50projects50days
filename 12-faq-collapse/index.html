Frequently Asked Questions

Where do lemurs live?

Lemurs are native to the African island of Madagascar

How many kinds of lemur are there?

There are over 100 known lemur species

What kind of lemur is King Julien from Madagascar?

King Julien is a ring-tailed lemur

How did lemurs get to Madagascar?

Lemurs arrived on Madagascar on floating islands of vegetation more than 40 million years ago

What's the biggest species of lemur?

The Indri is the largest type of lemur still living. Fossils found of the Archaeoindris, the world's largest lemur are comparable in size to a male gorilla.
